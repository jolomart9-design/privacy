03 · Data Usage
How We Use Your Information

All data entered into Expense Tracker is used solely to provide you with the app's features:

  • Display your income and expense transaction history
  • Calculate total expenses and income summaries
  • Generate expense charts and category breakdowns
  • Track your budget and show remaining balance
  • Remember your preferred display language
  • Show ads via internet connection (the only use of internet in this app)

We never use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than running the app features you see on screen.

💾
04 · Data Storage
Where Your Data Lives

All your financial data is stored locally on your device using Android's built-in local database (SQLite / SharedPreferences). This means:

  • No data is ever uploaded to our servers or any cloud service
  • The app works fully offline — no internet connection required for core features
  • We have no access to your data — not even we can see it
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data (unless backed up)
🗄️
05 · Backup & Restore
Keep Your Data Safe

Expense Tracker includes a built-in Backup & Restore feature that allows you to save and recover your financial data. This feature has three options:

  • 💾 Take Backup — exports all your transaction data as a JSON file and saves it to your phone's Downloads folder. No internet required.
  • 🔄 Restore Data — imports a previously saved backup JSON file from your device storage and restores all your transactions. No internet required.
  • 👁️ View Backup — lets you preview the contents of a backup file before restoring. No internet required.
🔒 Privacy Note: The backup file is saved only on your device (Downloads folder). It is never uploaded to any server or cloud. You are fully in control of where you store or share this file.
⚠️ Important Warning: If you uninstall the app without taking a backup, all your data will be permanently lost. We strongly recommend taking a backup regularly, especially before uninstalling or changing your phone.
